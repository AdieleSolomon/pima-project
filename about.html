Who We Are

Built for Practical Careers, Not Just Certificates

PIMA supports degree holders, undergraduates, O'Level graduates, and non-certificate learners with structured pathways into high-demand technical fields.

Our Founding Purpose

The institute was founded to close the gap between education and employability by creating immersive, workshop-first training programs. Learners graduate with demonstrable competence, safety discipline, and market confidence.

Our Distinction

We blend structured technical curriculum, entrepreneurship coaching, and field-based project execution to ensure every trainee can either secure work or launch a business immediately after completion.

Institutional Direction

Vision, Mission, and Philosophy

Vision

To become the leading applied technical institute in Africa for training industry-ready professionals, innovators, and entrepreneurs.

Mission

Deliver modern, practical, and technology-backed training that empowers learners to solve real-world problems and compete globally.

Philosophy

Competence over theory alone. Every learner must build, troubleshoot, and deploy tangible outcomes before graduation.
