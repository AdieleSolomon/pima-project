About PIMA TRAINING INSTITUTE

Building practical careers through technical and vocational training

Discover our mission, vision, and the team dedicated to your success in practical technical careers

Our Mission

PIMA TRAINING INSTITUTE is a practical training ground for degree holders, undergraduates, O'Level certificate holders, and even learners with no certificate. We focus on hands-on technical and vocational training that leads directly to employability, self-employment, and sustainable livelihoods.

Our Vision

To become a leading technical and vocational institute in Nigeria, producing highly skilled artisans and technicians who solve practical problems, create jobs, and support national development.

Why Choose PIMA TRAINING INSTITUTE Technical Programs?

  • Entry paths for degree holders, undergraduates, O'Level holders, and non-certificate learners
  • Hands-on, workshop-based learning focused on practical competence
  • Programs aligned with real market demand in technical trades
  • Experienced trainers from industry and field practice
  • Business/startup guidance for self-employment after training
  • Flexible schedules for full-time and working learners
  • Safety, quality, and professionalism embedded in each track
  • Pathways to employment, apprenticeship, and entrepreneurship

Our Team

Our dedicated team of trainers and industry professionals is committed to helping every learner build practical skills and a sustainable technical career path.
