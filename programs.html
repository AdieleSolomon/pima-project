Core Programs

Hands-On Technical Career Tracks

Every program combines theory, practical workshops, and business-focused project work.

Plumbing Installation

Domestic and commercial pipe systems, leak diagnostics, fittings, and maintenance.

Wall & Floor Tiling

Site measurement, layout design, tile finishing, and quality control standards.

Tailoring & Fashion

Pattern drafting, garment construction, machine mastery, and finishing techniques.

Electrical Installation

Safe wiring practices, load calculations, electrical troubleshooting, and repair.

Carpentry & Woodwork

Furniture making, framing systems, power tool handling, and finishing quality.

Welding & Fabrication

Arc welding, metal cutting, fabrication drawings, and workshop safety compliance.

Painting & Finishes

Surface prep, decorative treatments, color finishing, and coating system selection.

HVAC & Refrigeration

Cooling systems installation, diagnostics, maintenance, and energy optimization.

Automobile Mechanics

Engine diagnostics, brake systems, servicing routines, and workshop operations.

Solar Installation

Panel configuration, inverter systems, battery management, and off-grid solutions.

Masonry & Blocklaying

Structural blockwork, concrete handling, and site finishing standards.

Furniture Upholstery

Frame restoration, foam design, upholstery finishing, and client-ready delivery.

Admission Track

How to Join PIMA

1. Choose Program

Select your preferred technical track based on career objective.

2. Submit Application

Complete your online application and upload required details.

3. Screening & Guidance

Admissions team reviews your profile and recommends study pathway.

4. Begin Training

Start intensive practical training with your assigned cohort.
