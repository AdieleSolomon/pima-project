Build Real-World
Technical Skills

PIMA TRAINING INSTITUTE prepares learners for practical, income-generating careers in high-demand technical fields. Whether you are a degree holder, undergraduate, O'Level graduate, or have no certificate, you can start and grow here.

Open to: Degree holders, undergraduates, O'Level holders, and learners with no certificate.

Plumbing Installation

Train in pipe fitting, bathroom installations, water systems, and practical maintenance for homes and commercial buildings.

Wall & Floor Tiling

Learn accurate measurement, tile layout, cutting, grouting, and finishing for residential and site projects.

Tailoring & Fashion Production

Master pattern drafting, machine operation, fabric cutting, garment finishing, and customer-ready outfits.

Electrical Installation

Gain hands-on skills in safe wiring, domestic circuits, fault detection, and power distribution basics.

Carpentry & Woodwork

Build practical competencies in furniture making, framing, measurement, finishing, and workshop safety.

Welding & Metal Fabrication

Practice cutting, joining, and shaping metal components with focus on quality, safety, and employability.

Painting & Finishes

Learn surface preparation, coating systems, and decorative finishing techniques for homes and commercial spaces.

HVAC & Refrigeration

Gain practical skills in cooling systems, installation, troubleshooting, and preventive maintenance.

Automobile Mechanics

Train in diagnostics, servicing, and repair for engines, braking systems, and routine vehicle maintenance.

Solar Installation

Install and maintain solar panels, inverters, and battery systems for reliable alternative energy solutions.
